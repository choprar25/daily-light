Privacy Policy Daily Light — Last updated: 14 June 2026 Summary Daily Light does not collect, store, or share any personal data on any server. Everything the app needs is kept only on your own device. What information is used When you first open the app, you enter your date of birth so the app can show your star sign and a daily quote and tip. This date of birth, along with your reminder time preference, is stored only in your device's local app storage. This information is never transmitted to us, to the app developer, or to any third party. We do not have access to it. Data sharing Daily Light does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect or transmit any data in the first place. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Notifications Daily Light can send you push notifications with your daily quote and tip, a few times a day, even when the app is closed. To do this, the app uses a notification service called OneSignal. If you allow notifications, your device is given an anonymous subscription ID, which is shared with OneSignal so it can deliver these notifications to your device. This ID is not linked to your name, date of birth, or any other personal information, and is only used to deliver the app's daily notifications. You can stop this at any time by turning off notifications for the app in your device's settings. Children's privacy Daily Light does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because no information is collected at all. Changes to this policy If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds new features that use data differently — this page will be updated and the "last updated" date above will change accordingly. Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer through the contact details listed on this app's Google Play Store page.
