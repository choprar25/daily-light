Privacy Policy

Daily Light — Last updated: 14 June 2026

Summary

Daily Light does not collect, store, or share any personal data on any server. Everything the app needs is kept only on your own device.

What information is used

When you first open the app, you enter your date of birth so the app can show your star sign and a daily quote and tip. This date of birth, along with your reminder time preference, is stored only in your device's local app storage.

This information is never transmitted to us, to the app developer, or to any third party. We do not have access to it.

Data sharing

Daily Light does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect or transmit any data in the first place. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the notification is generated and shown by your device. No notification content or schedule is sent to any external server.

Children's privacy

Daily Light does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because no information is collected at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds new features that use data differently — this page will be updated and the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer through the contact details listed on this app's Google Play Store page.
